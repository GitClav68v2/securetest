Who We Are

Integration One is a California company that helps small and mid-size businesses design and coordinate professional perimeter security systems. Our website is integrationone.net.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains in plain terms what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

What We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily give us through our quote request form. That includes:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your city and state
  • Your business type and what you're looking to protect
  • Any additional details you choose to share in the message field

We don't use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or any third-party advertising scripts on this site.

Why We Collect It

The only reason we ask for your information is to respond to your quote request. We use it to:

  • Understand your security needs and put together a tailored recommendation
  • Match you with a vetted installer in your area
  • Follow up with you about your project

We don't use your information for marketing, and we don't add you to any mailing lists without your consent.

How Your Form Submission Is Processed

Our quote form is powered by Web3Forms, a third-party form submission service. When you submit a quote request, your information is transmitted through Web3Forms and delivered to our inbox. Web3Forms has its own privacy policy which you can review on their website.

We do not store your form submissions in any database on our end — they come directly to us as email.

We Don't Sell or Share Your Information

Full stop. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. The only people who see your submission are the Integration One team working on your quote.

We may share your project details with an installer we're matching you with — but only with your knowledge as part of the service you requested.

How We Protect Your Information

Our site is served over HTTPS, so any information you send through the form is encrypted in transit. We keep your information only as long as needed to complete your request or maintain our business relationship with you.

Your Rights

As a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we've collected and to request that we delete it.

In practice, if you've submitted a quote request and want us to delete your information, just email us and we'll take care of it.

Questions or Requests

If you have any questions about this policy or want to request that we delete your information, reach out anytime:
