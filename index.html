Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers to the things businesses ask us most.

Do I have to use your monitoring service?

No — you choose your own monitoring provider. We help connect your system to any professional monitoring center you prefer. No bundled contract, no lock-in.

What camera and sensor brands do you work with?

Any brand you trust. We're completely hardware-agnostic — Axis, Hanwha, Hikvision, Dahua, and more. You're never forced into a specific manufacturer.

How long does installation take?

Most installs are completed in one to two days depending on the size of the site. We'll give you a clearer timeline once we understand your setup.

What areas do you serve?

We work with businesses nationwide through our vetted installer network.

What if I already have some security equipment?

No problem — we can design around existing hardware and expand your current setup rather than start from scratch.

How do you vet your installers?

We work only with licensed, insured low-voltage professionals. Each installer in our network is reviewed before we send them to your site.

What does a typical system cost?

Every site is different, which is why we offer a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs and budget.
